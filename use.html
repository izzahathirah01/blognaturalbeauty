WAYS TO USE HOME REMEDIES TO TREAT EYE BAGS
REMEDY METHOD
TEA BAG 1. Steep two tea bags in hot water. 2. Squeeze out the liquid from the tea bags
3. To use as a warm compress, wait for them to cool down slightly, or to use as a cold compress, cool them in the refrigerator.
4. Place the tea bags on closed eyes for up to 15 minutes.
5. Repeat as often as needed. Best to use green tea bags
ALMOND 1. Mix almond with any oil that you have, or just simply buy almond oil.
2. You can directly apply almond oil on your dark circles. It won't harm your under-eye skin
3. ake three-four drops of almond oil on your fingers tips. Apply evenly all over your dark circles before going to bed.
4. Rinse it next in the morning with lukewarm water.
5. Repeat this process regularly for effective results.
